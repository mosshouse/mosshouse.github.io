Commercial

We manage tight deadlines and budgets for both large and small commercial buildings, interiors and exteriors. Just a few of our commercial services include:

  • Office buildings & schools
  • Hotels & retail space
  • Warehouse touchups and refinishes
  • Medical facilities
  • Interior and exterior commercial painting

Safety and Insurance

We are fully insured and maintain the highest level of safety during your commercial painting project. Where required, our employees also use proper harness and safety equipment to eliminate any chance of a workplace incident.

Flexibility – Nights & Weekends. We work around you and your business

Downtime costs money, which is why we work around your schedule. From project start to finish, our crews are courteous, professional and punctual. We make commercial painting a hassle-free experience.

Planning, Executing and Delivery

Our commercial painting process starts with a meeting and initial assessment. When involved in the beginning, we can often make cost-effective recommendations in reducing time and materials involved in the painting and finishing process. Our process looks like this:

  • Communicate – Listen to your project needs, deadlines and requirements.
  • Plan & Price – We construct a detailed budget and schedule.
  • Execution – The project starts. We work around you and your employees. Flexibility is crucial.
  • Follow up & Sign off – We complete a final walkthrough and inspection with you to ensure it meets your standards.

Residential

Read about the Almax residential painting and decorating process from consultation to after sales care. Show More

We can offer advice on what colours best accent certain areas or walls of your home. If you already have your colours selected we can simply provide you with an estimate and time frame to complete your project. Either way, we adapt to meet your needs.

From start to finish, you can expect detail and professionalism. Almax professionals always perform and use the following:

  1. Full preperation and sanding of imperfections and other interior surfaces to ensure a flawless base in which to apply your new paint.
  2. Large drop clothes used throughout to protect your flooring and spaces.
  3. High quality low-VOC paints can be supplied to reduce odours and smells

Post-paint, clean-up, inspection and completion

Once our team are fully finished we then take you on a walk-through and address any questions or concerns you may have.
