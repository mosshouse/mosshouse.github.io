Location

The Moss House is situated in the heart of Bunclody, just 180 meters from the Main Street, and adjacent to Bunclody Golf and Fishing Club. It's approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes from the South side of Dublin City and an hour from Rosslare ferry port.

Address:

N80, Carrigduff, Bunclody, Co. Wexford

GPS co-ordinates:

52.656199, -6.657660

Directions from Dublin:

  1. From Dublin take the N7 (Naas Road). At Junction 9, continue forward onto the M7 motorway (signposted Limerick)
  2. Leave the M7 at Junction 11, then join the M9 (Signposted Waterford)
  3. Leave the M9 at Junction 5, then at the roundabout take the 1st exit onto the N80 (Signposted Rosslare)
  4. Continue forward on the N80 entering Ballon.
  5. Continue forward for on the N80 for approximately 12 Kilometers and enter Bunclody.

From Rosslare Ferry Port

  1. From Rosslare Ferry Port take the N25, entering Tagoat.
  2. At Rosslare roundabout take the 1st exit onto the N25 (signposted Cork) onto Wexford Bypass.
  3. At Duncannon road roundabout take the 2nd exit onto the N25 (signposted Cork)
  4. AT New Ross road take the 2nd exit onto the N11 (Signposted Dublin) .
  5. Continue forward on the N11 entering Oilgate and then Enniscorthy.
  6. Bear left in Enniscorthy (one way system) signposted all routes.
  7. Bear left onto the N11 (Signposted Dublin, Carlow N80)
  8. AT roundabout take the 2nd exit onto the Dublin Road – N11 (Signposted Dublin, Carlow N80)
  9. Continue for approximately 14 kilometers and enter Bunclody
