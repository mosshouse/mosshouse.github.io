Rooms & Facilities

Rooms

There are six bedrooms, ranging from standard twin rooms to larger rooms with two double beds. Five bedrooms are en-suite. The sixth bedroom has a private bathroom, just a few steps outside the room.

Facilities

Facilities include a fully fitted kitchen, complete with oven, microwave, fridge. The kitchen’s dining area can comfortably seat up to six people. Breakfast cereals are included.

Parking

Spacious private parking on premises.
